Drime

Lead Ruby on Rails /JS Developer

February 2021 - April 2024 - Permanent contract - Full remote

www.drime.co

DRIME is a responsive, multilingual, interactive, and embeddable video player without cookies, consisting of two main applications: the Drime player and the associated DRIME video format, as well as the Drime video editor.

The .drime format is a JSON format that allows the video(s) to play in all the languages desired by the client with subtitles, voiceovers, and dynamic designs according to the language, all while using only one video file. This reduces time, storage costs, and the ecological footprint related to file storage; regardless of the number of desired languages, only one video is hosted and a Drime file is created per project.


1/ The Drime player and the .drime file format:

- For professionals/clients:

Uploading video tutorials shot by the DRIME team to be optimized for playback on all screen resolutions.

The ability to broadcast the video in multiple languages thanks to voiceover, subtitles, and dynamic video designs available in all languages chosen by the client, as well as voice commands. It is also possible to break down the tutorials/manuals into steps for step-by-step reading.

In addition to its video library available for playback on the DRIME site in its dedicated space, the client can also embed one or more videos or their entire library on their web page or elsewhere through iframes (indexed by Google).

Dashboard with Drime "Smart Indicators," user rights management, customized iframes, customized QR Codes. Soon, dynamic video designs will be available.


- For users:

A video playback platform that adapts to the resolution of their screen with the possibility of having videos in "step-by-step" mode or continuous playback with voice commands, sound adjustment, size and presence of subtitles, and depending on the type of video, the choice of language. By default, the user will have access to videos in their browser's language where possible or otherwise in English.

2/ The Drime video editor:

The Drime video editor is somewhat different from the basic player. It allows the client to create a slideshow from short videos and/or photos by choosing transition effects similar to the Drime player, i.e., multilingual, responsive, interactive, embeddable, and lightweight.

If you know which media you want to use and the texts you want to associate with them in your language, in five minutes you have created and published your video, and if you take 2 more minutes, you add a language to the video (voice-over, subtitles, etc.). You can also insert your logo and a soundtrack.

In short, with a bit of preparation, you can create your video in 10 different languages in ten minutes, and it is directly available online. Then you can export it either in iframe, by inserting a line of code directly on your site, or on the Drime site with its URL.
